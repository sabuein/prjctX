We offer a range of plans to cater to the needs of all types of art collectors. Our packages are designed to give you the best experience possible, so you can focus on building your collection. If you find that our packages are too small for your needs, please contact us for an individual offer for bigger packages.

All our plans come with the following features:

  • Secure online access to your collection
  • Ability to create and manage custom collections
  • Import and export data to and from your collection
  • Unlimited storage for images and documents
  • Mobile and tablet-friendly access

Here are the add-ons you can choose to enhance your plan:

  1. Premium Support: For $50/month, you'll receive priority support from our team of experts. They'll be available to assist you with any questions or issues you may have.
  2. Advanced Reporting: For $100/month, you can access advanced reporting features that will help you track the value of your collection over time. You'll be able to generate custom reports that show you how your collection is performing.
  3. Social Sharing: For $75/month, you can share your collection on social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter. This will allow you to showcase your collection to a wider audience.
  4. Custom Design: For $200/month, you can get a custom design for your collection. Our team of designers will work with you to create a unique and personalized design that reflects your style and taste.
  5. Art Authentication: For $150/month, we'll provide authentication services for your collection. Our team of experts will verify the authenticity of your artwork and provide you with a certificate of authenticity.

Please note that these prices do not include taxes. If you have any questions about our plans or add-ons, please feel free to contact us. We're here to help you build your collection!
